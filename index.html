Machine History

This HAAS DT-1 has an exceptional provenance. Built as a demonstration unit for Landré (the official HAAS distributor in the Netherlands), it spent its early years in their professional showroom before being acquired by IMBU BV for prototyping work.

August 2018
Manufactured by HAAS
Built as a demonstration machine for Landré's professional showroom in the Netherlands.
2018 - 2021
Showroom Demo Unit
Operated in climate-controlled showroom conditions at Landré, demonstrating capabilities to prospective HAAS customers.
October 2021
Acquired by IMBU BV
Purchased by IMBU BV for R&D prototyping support. Never used for production runs.
2021 - 2025
Prototyping Use Only
Used exclusively for prototyping and development work. No production cycles, resulting in exceptionally low spindle hours for its age.
December 1, 2025
Full Service by Landré
Complete service and inspection by authorized HAAS dealer. Service report available upon request.
